London Data Week 2025

Author: Jen Ding

After a successful London Data Week (LDW) 2023 and 2024, through a partnership with the GLA, LOTI, the Turing Institute, and more, Jen will build up a team of volunteers to further amplify the London data community for LDW25.

The Call for Applications will open in March 2025, but until then, please fill out this Expression of Interest form to share your interest.
